Privacy Policy

Pool Wizard Spellbook  ·  Last updated: May 2026  ·  Governing law: State of Kansas

This Privacy Policy describes how Pool Wizard Spellbook ("the app") handles information when you use the app at themysticturtle.github.io/pool-wizard/. Pool Wizard is an independent solo-developer project.

1. Information Collected

No personal information is collected. Pool Wizard Spellbook does not require an account, login, or registration. No data you enter into the app — including pool readings, pool profile settings, or history — is transmitted to any server. All of this data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage on your own device.

2. Local Storage

The app uses your browser's localStorage to save the following data on your device only:

  • Your pool profile (pool type, gallonage, sanitizer preferences)
  • Your pool chemistry history (readings you choose to save)
  • App preferences (compact mode, unit preferences)
  • A record that you have accepted these terms

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to me or any third party.

3. Advertising — Google AdSense

This app displays advertisements served by Google AdSense, a service provided by Google LLC. Google AdSense may use cookies, browser identifiers, and other tracking technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this app and/or other sites on the Internet. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, not this policy.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting adssettings.google.com. You can also opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info/choices.

4. Affiliate Links

This app may contain affiliate links to third-party retailers. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, I may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. Affiliate links are clearly labeled within the app. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party sites you visit through these links.

5. Cookies

Pool Wizard Spellbook itself does not set cookies. Google AdSense may set cookies on your device for the purposes described in Section 3. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though doing so may affect ad functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and is intended for use by adults who maintain residential swimming pools. No information from children under 13 is knowingly collected. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this app, please reach out via GitHub so appropriate action can be taken.

7. Do Not Track

Pool Wizard Spellbook itself does not track users and does not respond differently to Do Not Track signals. However, Google AdSense operates independently and may not honor Do Not Track signals. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of the State of Kansas, without regard to conflict of law principles.
