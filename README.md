# Pool Wizard

Pool Wizard is a lightweight pool chemistry companion for homeowners who want clear, practical guidance without handing their pool data to a cloud service.

Use it to save a pool profile, enter test readings, review suggested chemical adjustments, and keep a local history of your water care over time.

## Lightweight and Private

Pool Wizard runs as a web app, but it behaves like a small installable app on your phone.

- The app is only a few megabytes.
- It can be saved to your home screen.
- It checks for updates when you open it.
- After loading, it can keep working offline.
- Your pool profile and history stay in your browser's local storage.
- No account is required.
- No pool history is uploaded to a server.

If you clear your browser data or uninstall the web app shortcut, your saved local data may be removed by your device.

## Built for Fast Field Use

Pool Wizard focuses on the routine decisions pool owners make again and again:

- Free chlorine, pH, alkalinity, CYA, calcium hardness, and salt targets
- CYA-aware chlorine recommendations
- Algae and slime treatment flows
- Chemical side effects
- Saved readings and trend history
- A built-in reference spellbook for common pool care problems

The calculator is meant to support good testing habits, not replace judgment. Always retest after dosing, follow product labels, and be careful when handling pool chemicals.

## Ads and Support

Pool Wizard may show small ad banners in out-of-the-way areas of the interface.

The app is designed to keep working without ads, and the ads are not required for the calculator or saved history. When ads are available, they help generate a little revenue to support hosting, development time, testing, and future updates.
